History About NaPaul

NaPaul Pharmaceuticals is an accredited medical and pharmaceuticals wholesale distributors based in Ernakulam in Kerala, India with services covering most of Malappuram, Palakkad, Thrissur and Kottayam districts.

We are a licensed wholesale distributor of prescription / branded and generic medicines as well as dental healthcare medicines, animal/pet health medicines, etc. We also provide medical consumables, disposable gloves, and masks, medical diagnostic medicines and chemicals, surgical items and vaccines.
